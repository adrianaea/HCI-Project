Pepe's Cantina

Pepe’s Cantina is the kind of spot you hit once and instantly know you’ll be back. The Tacos de Carne are packed with flavor — perfectly seasoned, juicy, and served on fresh, warm tortillas. And don’t even get me started on the Tacos de Birria — rich, tender, and dripping in that savory consommé that you’ll want to drink straight up. Wash it all down with their signature Guava-Rita — a tropical twist on the classic margarita that’s refreshing, tangy, and totally addictive. This place is a whole vibe.

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

Ford's Garage

Buckle up for an unforgettable meal — Ford’s Garage blends vintage vibes with bold flavors. The Famous Firestone Shrimp comes out golden and crispy, with just the right amount of spice to start things off strong. For the main event, the Model "A" burger delivers in a big way: juicy, stacked high, and dripping with flavor. And don’t sleep on the Funnel Fries — warm, sugary perfection that tastes like a fairground classic. Combine all this with the garage-themed decor, and you’ve got a must-visit spot for burger lovers.

Texas Roadhouse

Texas Roadhouse knows how to serve up comfort with a kick. The Cactus Blossom is a crunchy, golden-fried appetizer that’s big enough to share—though you might not want to. The Ft. Worth Ribeye is everything a steak should be: tender, juicy, and grilled to smoky perfection. Pair it with their cinnamon butter rolls and you’re in heaven. To wash it all down, the Island Cooler adds a sweet tropical twist that’s refreshing without being too heavy. Loud, lively, and full of flavor—just how you want a roadhouse night to go.
